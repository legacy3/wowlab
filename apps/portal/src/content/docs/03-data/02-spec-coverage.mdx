## What "implemented" means

A fully implemented spec has:

- All rotational abilities
- All talent modifiers
- Proc handlers
- Pet abilities (if applicable)
- Tier set bonuses

## Coverage details

For implemented specs, coverage means:

- **Spells**: Core rotational abilities work
- **Talents**: Talent effects modify damage correctly
- **Procs**: RPPM and fixed-chance procs trigger
- **Cooldowns**: Cooldown reduction effects work
- **Resources**: Generation and costs are accurate

## Limitations

Even implemented specs may have limitations:

- Some utility abilities may not be simulated
- Complex interactions might be simplified
- Very new patches may have missing tuning data

Check [Known Issues](/dev/docs/04-engine/05-known-issues) for specific limitations.
